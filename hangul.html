Learning Hangul
What is Hangul?

Hangul is the Korean alphabet. Letters combine into syllable blocks.

Example: ㅎ + ㅏ + ㄴ → 한

Final Consonant (Batchim)

박 = ㅂ + ㅏ + ㄱ

Final sounds are shorter and clipped.

ㄱ, ㅋ → k | ㄷ, ㅅ → t | ㅂ → p

Reading Rules
  • Stop sound → 박 = ba(k)
  • Liaison → 한국어 = han-gu-geo
  • ㅇ silent at start, “ng” at end
